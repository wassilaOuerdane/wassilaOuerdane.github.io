About me

Welcom to my Web site. I am an Assistant Professor (Lecturer, MCF) in Computer Science at CentraleSupélec, and a member of the MICS (Mathématiques et Informatique pour la Complexité et les Systèmes) Lab, within the Research Group LOGIMICS (Formal methods in computer science and knowledge modelling) since February 2019. Before this, I was in the LGI Lab at CentraleSupélec (2010-2018). I hold a PhD on Computer Science from University of Paris Dauphine in December 2009, on the subject "Multiple Criteria Decision Aiding: a dialectical Perspective".
Topics of Interests:
  • Multiple Criteria Decision aiding
  • Decision Support Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence (Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, Argumentation theory, Dialogue games)
